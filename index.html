How does NFT Stack work?

1. Creators generate their digital artwork



2. Creators mint limited edition copies of their digital art. Let say 500 copies.



3. Limited Edition copies of NFT are inserted into same number of stacks. At anytime every copy of stack will be identical.



4. Creator lists these stack copies on exchange under their Creator Account, giving Initial Stack Offering just like an IPO.


5. This allows trading of stacks similar to stocks. Upon buying a stack, the onwership of underlying NFT copies in the stack will transfer to the buyer.



6. Creator recieves as small royalty as a token of appreciation upon each transaction (0.05%).



7. Creator creates new Artwork, it will get added to every stack on our exchange automatically.



8. The new Artwork copies added to the stacks offer potential price of the stack to go up or down depending on whether the new Artwork is a value add.



9. New Artwork copies will be reflected in all the stacks of that creator on our exchange offering complete ease of trading.



10. Investors holding positions in stacks of creators will automatically get the ownership of this new Artwork copy. This allows investor to invest not just in the Digital Artwork but the Artist/Creator itself by judging the future potential of the creator.



11. Unlike other marketplaces, here every creator will have only 1 collectible, the stack of creator, but there can be potentially infinite Artworks associated with it. Multiple but limited number of copies of this stack provides ease of trading and the required liquidity.

Creators, Traders or Investors, why should you be interested?

Value for Creators

Initial Stack offering (ISO) allows creators to gain value by selling their stacks to buyers on our exchange.

Trustable for Investors

Lock In period prevents stacks from being sold immediately by the Creator after ISO when the stack prices may be artificially high and take away economic motivations from Creator to continue.

Incentive for Creators

Royalty fee paid to creator for each transaction and potential increase in the price of individual stacks over time, gives incentives for Creators to continue adding new Artworks.

Promotion and Trends

An inbuilt social platform allows Creators to interact with the community and create new trends.

Proof of Stake Qualification

Only Creators having significant history with the number of recurring trades of their minted NFT will be allowed to list for ISO.

Wallets

Support of multiple wallets to connect account and trade in decentralised manner will help operate in trustless manner.
