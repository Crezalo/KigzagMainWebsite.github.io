Loyalty Tokens for Newbies





1. Creator Tokens : fans and followers can buy creator tokens at price fixed by creator or community. These tokens should be treated as vouchers or tickets which can be bought to gain access to premium stuff but one cannot sell them back directly. A creator can choose to create subscription by deducting some tokens from holder on regular basis.


2. NFT Sale against Creator Token : Creator/Community can decide upon price of NFT in creator tokens and list it for sale.


3. DAO : for Creator community governance. Handles community treasury.
